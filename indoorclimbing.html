Indoor Climbing

Training indoors is one of the best ways to build the strength, technique, and confidence you need for outdoor climbing.

Tips for First-Timers

  • Most gyms offer day passes and rental gear — no need to own equipment to get started.
  • Ask about intro classes or a belay certification course before your first visit.
  • Start on the bouldering wall to get comfortable with movement before moving to ropes.
  • Comfortable athletic clothing and closed-toe shoes work fine until you get your first pair of climbing shoes.
